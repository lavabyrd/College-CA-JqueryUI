Situated in the heart of Galway, Gillian's B&B is a special slice of heaven. A stones throw from Eyre Square and more restaurants and traditional irish pubs than you can imagine. Couple that with the close coastal walks, breath taking scenoria, Gillian's B&B promises a special and humble slice of Ireland.

Awarded the TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence in both 2015 and 2016, you can be sure that your stay will be everything you need to be. With free wi-fi, free private off-street parking, full breakfast and all the usual ammenities, your stay will be relaxing, prepared and catered for.
